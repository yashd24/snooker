King's Snooker venues operate on a membership system. Memberships can be obtained by visiting either club, anytime during our opening hours.

In order to become a member, customers must provide:

  • Photographic ID (e.g., Passport, Driving Licence)
  • Proof of address (e.g., Passport,Other documents)
  • Passport Size Photograph

Membership fee is a one-off payment of ₹50000 for 1 year!

Members are free to visit both our other clubs, there is no need for separate memberships for each club.

Members are allowed to bring up to a maximum of 20 guests at a time. Each guest will be required to pay a guest fee of ₹1000.
Food will be an additional cost for guest members!
