OUR STORY
Kings's Snooker's Club is a family-run business and has been for over 30 years.
The club is a great place to unwind and play games with friends and colleagues.
Open 7 days a week 11am until late. We have a competitively priced fully licensed bar and serve great food.
We have 11 snooker tables and six English pool tables.
